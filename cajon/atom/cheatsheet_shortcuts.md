#### Cheat-sheet Shortcuts

| Functions                         | Shortcuts                 |
|:----------------------------------|:-------------------------:|
|Uppercase                          | `ctrl + k` then `ctrl + u`|
|Lowercase                          | `ctrl + k` then `ctrl + l`|
|Markdown preview                   | `ctrl + alt m`            |
|Split selection into lines         | `ctrl + shift + L`        |
|Merge selection into a single line | `ctrl + shift + M`        |
