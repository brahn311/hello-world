# Use too and either
We use too and either when:
we want to show that we have something in common with someone

We use the correspondent auxiliary to respond to the expression.

- I **love** Flowers
- I **do** too

- She **isn't** cold right now
- I **am not** either

- They **enjoy** cooking
- We **do** too

- He **has** a car
- She **does** too
